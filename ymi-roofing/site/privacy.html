Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Y.M.I Roofing Pty Ltd (ACN 695 710 055) respects your privacy and complies with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. This policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information when you use our website or contact us for roofing and cleaning services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and phone number (via our quote request form or phone call)
  • Suburb and address (to confirm service area and prepare quotes)
  • Details about your roofing or cleaning needs (service type, roof age, damage description)
  • Any messages or additional information you choose to share

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only to:

  • Contact you about your quote or enquiry
  • Provide roofing and cleaning services and arrange site visits
  • Send follow-up communications related to your job
  • Request Google reviews after job completion (with opt-out)
  • Maintain records of work performed for warranty and compliance purposes

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Australian Consumer Law, we are required to provide services with due care and skill. We collect and process your data as necessary to:

  • Perform our contract with you (the roofing or cleaning service)
  • Comply with legal obligations (building regulations, tax records)
  • Protect your vital interests (safety during emergency repairs)

4. Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We use:

  • Google Sheets for lead tracking (protected by Google account security)
  • Twilio for SMS notifications (encrypted in transit)
  • n8n automation platform (self-hosted, access-controlled)
  • Cloudflare for website hosting and DDoS protection

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security
  • Google Sheets — lead and job tracking
  • Twilio — SMS notifications to our team and customers
  • n8n — workflow automation
  • ManyChat — Facebook/Instagram messaging (if you contact us via social media)

These services may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum information necessary.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This collects anonymous data such as pages visited, time on site, and referral source. You can opt out of Google Analytics using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. SMS Communications & Opt-Out

After completing a job, we may send you a single SMS requesting a Google review. You can opt out of these messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any review request SMS
  • Emailing y.m.iroofing@outlook.com
  • Calling 0422 093 241

Once you opt out, we will not send you any further marketing or review communications.

8. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Consumer Law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal record-keeping requirements — we must retain job records for 7 years under Victorian building law)
  • Complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe we have mishandled your data

9. Complaints

If you have a complaint about our privacy practices, contact us first. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can lodge a complaint with:

  • Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) — oaic.gov.au · 1300 363 992
  • Consumer Affairs Victoria — consumer.vic.gov.au · 1300 55 81 81
