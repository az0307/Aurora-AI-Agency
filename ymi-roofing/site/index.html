Your Rights

Australian Consumer Law Protection

Y.M.I Roofing operates under the Australian Consumer Law. You have automatic rights on every job.

✓
Services With Due Care & Skill
Under ACL s 60, every roofing and cleaning job must be performed with due care and skill. If we fall short, we'll fix it at no cost.
✓
Fit for Purpose Guarantee
Under ACL s 61, our work must be fit for the purpose you told us about. If your roof still leaks after we repaired it, we come back.
✓
Completed Within Reasonable Time
Under ACL s 62, work must be completed within a reasonable time. We set clear start and finish dates before any work begins.
✓
No "No Refund" Policies
It's illegal to deny refunds for major failures. We don't use them. If there's a major problem, you choose: repair, replacement, or refund.

Cooling-Off
For door-to-door or unsolicited agreements. Full refund, no questions.

Quotes
